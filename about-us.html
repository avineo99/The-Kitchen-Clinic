About Us

We are a proud family-owned business which continues to build on our legacy with new products, advanced technology, and fresh design ideas. Unlike mass-produced options, our bespoke services allow you to customize everything from cupboard doors to full kitchen overhauls, including worktops, sinks, taps, and appliances. Our products offer unique styles and a perfect fit, saving time and money while maintaining the very best quality.

We have been transforming kitchens with high-quality designs, doors, drawers, worktops, and appliances while staying ahead of evolving trends.

Most people only renovate their kitchens occasionally due to the cost and disruption, which is why we provide an exceptional service working with leading brands like Neff, Bosch, and Samsung.

Whether you want a modern or traditional look, our bespoke solutions enhance the way your kitchen looks without the expense and time commitment of a full re-fit.

Renovating your kitchen is a major investment, and we ensure a smooth, stress-free experience. We keep you informed throughout the process using your preferred communication method, so you’re always in the loop.

Our trusted employees uphold the highest standards of service and professionalism, making sure every detail is handled with care. With countless satisfied customers and glowing recommendations, we remain the go-to brand for kitchen makeovers.

As your local experts in kitchen refurbishment, we have evolved into industry leaders by offering an alternative to complete kitchen replacements.

Our reputation for service, quality, and value has grown because we focus on more than just replacing doors and drawers—we ensure every detail is meticulously handled.

Whether you desire a full transformation or a minor update, our expertise and dedication sets us apart.

Our Commitment to Sustainability!

The Kitchen Clinic is committed to providing high-quality kitchen products that benefit both our customers and the environment. Sustainability is at the heart of our business, and we encourage customers to reuse existing cabinets, replacing only doors and drawers where possible. We ensure responsible disposal of old components through eco-friendly methods, reducing waste and minimizing our environmental impact.

Responsible Sourcing:  We partner with suppliers who share our sustainability values, and use MDF wood from managed forests wherever possible.

Eco-Friendly Makeovers: Replacing doors and drawers instead of full units conserves resources, while our durable products extend kitchen lifespans.

Waste Reduction & Recycling: We try and recycle as many of the removed kitchen materials as we can to minimize waste.

Continuous Improvement: We constantly refine our processes, integrating new eco-friendly practices to further reduce our footprint.

By prioritizing sustainability, we help customers create beautiful kitchens while protecting the planet for future generations.
